1.查询出2月底前的销售订单，至今未开发票的品种和数量

按分类更新，八个油墨分类全部修改为批次管理
—查询语句
select wholemanaflag
from bd_invmandoc man
left join bd_invbasdoc inv
on man.pk_invbasdoc = inv.pk_invbasdoc
left join bd_invcl invcl
on inv.pk_invcl = invcl.pk_invcl
where nvl(man.dr,0)=0
and invcl.invclasscode in (‘25013101’,’25013102’,’25013103’,’25013104’,’25013105’,’25013106’,’25013107’,’25013108’)
and man.pk_corp = ‘1097’;
—修改批次管理语句
UPDATE (select wholemanaflag
from bd_invmandoc man
left join bd_invbasdoc inv
on man.pk_invbasdoc = inv.pk_invbasdoc
left join bd_invcl invcl
on inv.pk_invcl = invcl.pk_invcl
where nvl(man.dr,0)=0
and invcl.invclasscode in (‘25013101’,’25013102’,’25013103’,’25013104’,’25013105’,’25013106’,’25013107’,’25013108’)
and man.pk_corp = ‘1097’) mm SET mm.wholemanaflag = ‘N’;
—提交
commit;
–说明：
–1.先执行查询语句，确认是要更新的数据及行数。
–2.执行更改批次管理语句。（若有异常，请不要提交,回滚至未执行态）
–3.提交。
